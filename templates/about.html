About

The coronavirus, also known as COVID-19, is currently an international pandemic with the current shortage in testing kits there is an important need for secondary means to filter those who are infected with the virus and those who may have another, potentially treatable condition.

Our goal is to use Artificial Intelligence while comparing accuracy using different loss functions and optimizers to detect COVID-19 in chest x-rays images.


The Team

Our team is composed of 5 SFU undergraduate students. We aim to help the world through devastation by creating bio medical related software.
